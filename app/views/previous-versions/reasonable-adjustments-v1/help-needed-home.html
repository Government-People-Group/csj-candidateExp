Help you need when applying for a job

Tell us about any help you might need during the application process.

You can add, remove or edit this information at any time.
What do we do with this information?

If you tell us you need help with parts of the application process, we will try and support you by making reasonable adjustments where possible.

Examples of reasonable adjustments include:

  • providing an application form in Braille if you have sight difficulties
  • giving you easy access to an interview room if you are on crutches or recovering from an operation
  • letting you have more time to complete assessments

You can also tell us if you do not need help but there's some information that might be useful for us to know. For example, you could tell us if you do not like to make eye contact with a person who's interviewing you and we would pass this information on to the person doing the interview.

! Telling us you need help applying does not affect how we assess your application in any way. You will not be penalised for asking for help from us.
