Preparing for your interview

When you arrive at the building, report to reception. Remember to arrive early so you can avoid any delays.

Your interview will be about 1 hour long. You'll be assessed on your interview presentation and your answers to our interview questions.

You can review your application and interview details on the Your applications section of your candidate portal.

Interview presentation

You'll give a 15-minute presentation on 'Managing relationships with key policy stakeholders'.

Your presentation should cover:

Stakeholder Analysis: Identify three key stakeholders and explain their influence and interests.

Engagement Strategy: Outline a clear strategy and specific tactics for engaging with each stakeholder.

Managing Challenges: Describe how you would handle a potential challenge, like a conflict of interest, and adapt your strategy.

Measuring Success: Explain how you would measure the success of your engagement efforts.
