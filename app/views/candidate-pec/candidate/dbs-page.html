DBS


This check is in progress

You do not need to do anything
What is this check?
It checks the criminal record of someone applying for a role. This role requires a Basic DBS check, which looks for unspent convictions and conditional cautions.

We can decline your employment offer if we find any criminal information on the DBS check that might affect your application.
How long will it take?
This check usually takes about two working days
What can make the check take longer than usual?
It is checked by a different department than most of the other checks. We do not know how busy they are.

If something is found in the check that needs to be investigated further, this will make the check take longer. You will be contacted if any further information is needed from you.
